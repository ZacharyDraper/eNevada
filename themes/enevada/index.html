Welcome to Intellitext

Providing Transcription, Video Captioning, Audio Transcription, Minutes and More.

Intellitext LLC is dedicated to providing quality real-time transcription to the deaf and hard of hearing, video captioning, audiofile transcription, dictation, and procedural minutes. We utilize TypeWell, a software program which allows transcribers to capture the meaning of what's spoken at rapid speeds. We customize each service to fit our client's diverse needs, as we understand that no two projects are the same.

TypeWell is used widely across the United States, and is accepted under ADA, IDEA and Section 504 of the Rehabilitation Act of 1973.
